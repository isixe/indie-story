Micro SaaS refers to small software services run by individuals or tiny teams. I believe it's the ideal business model for indie developers.

## What Is Micro SaaS

Characteristics:
- Solves a specific, small problem
- Clear, niche user base
- Lean but practical features
- High automation, low maintenance

## My Micro SaaS Portfolio

I currently run three:
1. **Email Template Library** - $800/mo
2. **API Monitoring Tool** - $1,200/mo
3. **SEO Analysis Plugin** - $600/mo

Total: $2,600/mo — enough to live on.

## Advantages

1. **Low risk**: Small investment, cheap to fail
2. **Sustainable**: Build once, earn long-term
3. **Stackable**: Keep adding new products
4. **Full control**: No funding, no external pressure

## How to Find Ideas

- Solve your own problems
- Find pain points in forums and communities
- Improve a single feature of an existing product
- Build vertical tools for specific industries

Micro SaaS won't make you a billionaire overnight, but it's a sustainable, indie-friendly business model.
