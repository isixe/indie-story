Once your indie dev income stabilizes, registering a company becomes necessary. Here's the process in China.

## Why Register

1. **Legal payments**: Required by App Store, Google Play, payment platforms
2. **Tax compliance**: Avoid personal income tax risks
3. **Brand building**: Professional image
4. **IP protection**: Safeguard your intellectual property

## Company Type

### Sole Proprietorship
- Pros: Simple registration, lower tax
- Cons: Unlimited liability, can't raise funding
- Best for: Low income, solo operation

### Limited Liability Company (LLC)
- Pros: Limited liability, scalable
- Cons: Complex registration, higher tax
- Best for: Partners, growth plans

## Registration Process

### 1. Name Approval
Prepare 3-5 names, check availability on the工商 bureau website.

### 2. Submit Documents
- Articles of association
- Shareholder ID
- Registered address proof
- Business scope

### 3. Get License
Collect your business license after approval.

### 4. Company Seals
Official seal, financial seal,法人 seal.

### 5. Bank Account
Open a corporate bank account.

### 6. Tax Registration
Register with tax bureau, apply for invoices.

## Budget

- Registration: ¥500-2,000 (DIY)
- Address rental: ¥2,000-5,000/year (if needed)
- Bookkeeping: ¥200-500/month
- Bank account: ¥0-500

## Tips

- Cover your business scope comprehensively (software dev, tech services, etc.)
- Registered address must be valid
- File taxes on time — even zero filings
- Keep all financial records

I recommend hiring a professional bookkeeping service so you can focus on your product.
