SEO is a vital free traffic channel for indie developers. Here are techniques I've battle-tested.

## SEO Fundamentals

### Keyword Research
Free tools:
- Google Keyword Planner
- Ubersuggest
- Answer The Public

Look for:
- Moderate search volume (100-1,000/mo)
- Low competition
- Product-relevant long-tail keywords

## Technical SEO

### 1. Site Speed
- Test with PageSpeed Insights
- Optimize images
- Enable CDN
- Reduce JavaScript

### 2. Mobile Optimization
- Responsive design
- Mobile speed optimization
- Touch-friendly interactions

### 3. Structured Data
Add JSON-LD markup for:
- Product info
- Reviews and ratings
- FAQ

## Content SEO

### 1. Create Quality Content
- Blog posts solving user problems
- Detailed product documentation
- Case studies and success stories

### 2. Content Optimization
- Keywords in titles
- Proper H-tag usage
- Internal linking
- Alt text on images

## Link Building

- Product directory submissions
- Industry forum participation
- Guest blogging
- Resource page partnerships

## Track Results

Use Google Search Console and Analytics to monitor:
- Ranking changes
- Click-through and impression rates
- User behavior data

SEO is a long game — stick with it for 3-6 months before expecting results.
