People always ask me: when should you quit your job to go full-time indie? Here's my story.

## My Situation

I started my side project in 2021 while working full-time. Nights and weekends building my own product. By mid-2023, side income overtook my salary, so I finally took the leap.

## Preparation

### Financial
- Saved at least 12 months of living expenses
- Side income stable above salary for 3+ months
- Clear revenue growth trajectory

### Mental
- Accept income instability
- Prepare for the long haul
- Find your independent work rhythm

### Skills
- Not just coding — product, marketing, design
- Build your personal brand and network
- Find a community to exchange ideas

## Life After Quitting

The good:
- Time freedom, work at your own pace
- Do what you love, more motivation
- Higher income ceiling

The challenges:
- Loneliness — you have to proactively socialize
- Self-discipline is non-negotiable
- No steady paycheck brings anxiety

## Advice

Don't quit impulsively. Prepare thoroughly. Side income is just one condition — the real question is whether you're ready to face everything alone.
