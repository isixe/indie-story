In March 2022, I launched my first SaaS product. Two years later, it brings in over $10,000 in monthly recurring revenue.

## Product Positioning

An inventory management tool for e-commerce sellers. I chose this niche because I previously worked in e-commerce and knew the pain points firsthand.

## Cold Start

In the first three months, I acquired initial users through:
- Sharing valuable content in relevant forums and communities
- Sending personalized emails to potential customers
- Offering free trials to collect feedback

## Growth Strategy

Zero spend on ads. Growth came from:
- SEO optimization and writing industry blog posts
- Word-of-mouth referrals
- Built-in viral mechanics

## Tech Stack

- Frontend: Next.js + Tailwind CSS
- Backend: Node.js + PostgreSQL
- Deployment: Vercel + Railway

One person can build something valuable — pick the right direction and keep iterating.
